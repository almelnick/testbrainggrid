# Skill: tear-sheet (S&P Global)
**Command:** `/tear-sheet`

## Purpose
Generate a one-page company tear sheet using S&P Global Market Intelligence data.

## Content
1. **Company Overview** — description, sector, headquarters, employees
2. **Stock Data** — price, 52-week range, market cap, EV, avg daily volume
3. **Financials** — LTM Revenue, EBITDA, Net Income, EPS; 3-year growth rates
4. **Valuation** — EV/Revenue, EV/EBITDA, P/E vs. peers and sector median
5. **Estimates** — current year and next year Revenue, EBITDA, EPS consensus
6. **Credit** — S&P rating, outlook, last rating action date
7. **Ownership** — top 5 institutional holders, short interest

## Output Format
- One-page summary (PDF-ready layout described in markdown)
- All figures as of most recent available date with date stamps

## Data Source
S&P Global Market Intelligence

## Disclaimer
For informational purposes only. Not investment advice.

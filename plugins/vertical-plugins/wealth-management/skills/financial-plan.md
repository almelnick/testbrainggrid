# Skill: financial-plan
**Command:** `/plan`

## Purpose
Draft a comprehensive financial plan for a wealth management client.

## Sections
1. **Executive Summary** — net worth snapshot, top goals, key recommendations
2. **Goal Inventory** — retirement, education, major purchases, charitable giving
3. **Cash Flow Analysis** — income, expenses, savings rate, surplus/deficit
4. **Net Worth Statement** — assets (liquid, retirement, real estate), liabilities
5. **Retirement Projection** — Monte Carlo simulation summary, projected income in retirement, shortfall/surplus
6. **Investment Strategy** — target allocation, risk profile, time horizon
7. **Tax Strategy** — tax-advantaged account optimization, Roth conversion opportunities, TLH strategy
8. **Insurance Review** — life, disability, long-term care, liability coverage adequacy
9. **Estate Planning** — will/trust status, beneficiary designations, gifting strategy
10. **Action Plan** — prioritized next steps with owner and due date

## Output Format
- Comprehensive plan document (structured sections)
- Goal probability table (Monte Carlo results by scenario)
- Net worth summary table
- 90-day action plan checklist

## Disclaimer
For informational purposes only. Not investment, tax, or legal advice. Requires advisor review and client sign-off.

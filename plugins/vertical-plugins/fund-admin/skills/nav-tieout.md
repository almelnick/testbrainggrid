# Skill: nav-tieout
**Command:** `/nav`

## Purpose
Perform NAV (Net Asset Value) tie-out for period-end close, reconciling the fund administrator's NAV to the GP's internal calculation.

## Steps
1. Obtain GA NAV statement and GP internal NAV calculation for the period.
2. Reconcile total assets: investment fair values, cash, receivables, prepaid.
3. Reconcile total liabilities: management fees payable, accruals, credit facility balance.
4. Confirm NAV per unit/LP interest matches across both sources.
5. Investigate and document all reconciling items.
6. Confirm allocation of income, expenses, and unrealized gain/loss to each LP.
7. Produce NAV tie-out sign-off package for CFO.

## Output Format
- NAV bridge: GA balance → GP balance, with each reconciling item
- LP allocation table: each LP's opening NAV, contributions, distributions, income allocation, closing NAV
- Sign-off checklist: all items cleared or escalated

## Disclaimer
Draft only. Does not set official NAV. Requires CFO and auditor sign-off.

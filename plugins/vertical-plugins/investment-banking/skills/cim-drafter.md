# Skill: cim-drafter
**Command:** `/cim`

## Purpose
Draft a Confidential Information Memorandum (CIM) for a sell-side M&A process.

## Sections
1. **Executive Summary** — investment highlights, transaction overview, key stats
2. **Business Overview** — history, products/services, customers, geographies
3. **Industry & Market** — TAM/SAM, growth drivers, competitive landscape
4. **Competitive Positioning** — differentiation, moats, market share
5. **Financial Performance** — 3-year historical IS, BS, CF; key metrics; EBITDA bridge
6. **Financial Projections** — 3–5 year projections with assumptions
7. **Management Team** — bios, tenure, track record
8. **Transaction Overview** — process timeline, data room access, bid instructions

## Output Format
- Structured Word/Markdown draft with section headers
- Placeholder tables for financials (populate from uploaded model)
- Cover page template

## Disclaimer
Draft only. Requires legal, compliance, and senior banker review before distribution.

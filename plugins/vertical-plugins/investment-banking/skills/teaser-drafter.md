# Skill: teaser-drafter
**Command:** `/teaser`

## Purpose
Draft a deal teaser (anonymous executive summary) for the early stage of a sell-side M&A process.

## Sections
1. **Transaction Overview** — deal type, brief business description (anonymized), size indication
2. **Investment Highlights** — 4–6 compelling bullet points (growth, margins, competitive moats)
3. **Financial Snapshot** — LTM Revenue, EBITDA, EBITDA margin, growth rate (no company name)
4. **Market Opportunity** — TAM, growth rate, positioning
5. **Transaction Process** — indicative timeline, next steps, NDA requirement

## Output Format
- 1–2 page PDF-ready document
- No company name or identifying information (until NDA signed)
- Banker contact details at bottom

## Disclaimer
Draft only. Requires senior banker and compliance review before distribution.

# Skill: initiation-report
**Command:** `/initiation`

## Purpose
Draft an initiation of coverage research report for a company.

## Sections
1. **Investment Summary** — rating, price target, key thesis (3 bullets)
2. **Company Overview** — business description, history, segments, geographies
3. **Industry Analysis** — market size, growth drivers, competitive dynamics
4. **Investment Thesis** — 3–5 reasons to own (or avoid) the stock
5. **Financial Model** — 5-year revenue/EBITDA/EPS projections with assumptions
6. **Valuation** — DCF, comps, and blended target price derivation
7. **Risks** — upside and downside risks to thesis and price target
8. **Management & Governance** — team track record, alignment, board quality
9. **ESG Considerations** — material ESG factors for the sector

## Output Format
- Full initiation report (15–25 pages equivalent)
- Financial summary table (historical + 5-year projections)
- Valuation summary (comps table + DCF bridge)
- Rating: Buy / Neutral / Sell with price target and upside/(downside)

## Disclaimer
Draft only. Not a recommendation. Requires analyst review and compliance approval before publication.

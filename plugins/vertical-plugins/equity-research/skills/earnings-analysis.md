# Skill: earnings-analysis
**Command:** `/earnings`

## Purpose
Analyze an earnings release or call transcript and produce a research note.

## Steps
1. Parse press release: revenue, EBITDA, EPS vs. consensus and prior year.
2. Identify beats/misses by segment.
3. Pull management commentary from call transcript (guidance, tone, key themes).
4. Assess guidance revision: raise/lower/maintain vs. street.
5. Update model estimates for current and forward periods.
6. Draft research note: headline, key takeaways, estimate changes, valuation update.

## Output Format
- Earnings scorecard (actual vs. consensus vs. prior)
- Estimate revision table (old vs. new)
- Research note draft (1–2 pages)

## Data Sources
- Aiera (transcripts), FactSet (consensus estimates), S&P Global (filing data)

## Disclaimer
Draft only. Not a recommendation. Requires analyst review.

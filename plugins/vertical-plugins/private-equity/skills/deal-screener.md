# Skill: deal-screener
**Command:** `/screen`

## Purpose
Screen a company against a private equity fund's investment criteria to produce a preliminary investment assessment.

## Steps
1. Collect company overview: sector, business model, revenue, EBITDA, growth.
2. Apply fund investment criteria: sector fit, size (revenue/EBITDA), geography, growth profile, margin profile.
3. Assess business quality: revenue visibility, customer concentration, competitive moat.
4. Estimate entry multiple range and implied IRR at various exit assumptions.
5. Identify key diligence questions and red flags.
6. Score: Pass / Conditional Pass / Pass with Concerns / Fail.

## Output Format
- Investment criteria scorecard (each criterion: pass/fail/partial)
- Preliminary financial summary
- Preliminary returns framework (entry multiple, exit multiple, hold period, implied IRR)
- Top 5 diligence questions
- Screening recommendation

## Disclaimer
Preliminary screen only. Not a formal investment recommendation.
